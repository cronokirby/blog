Abstract

Secret sharing schemes represent a crucial cryptographic protocol, with linear codes serving as a primary tool for their construction. This paper systematically investigates the construction of ideal secret sharing schemes for complete ttt-partite kkk-uniform hypergraph access structures using linear codes as the tool. First, it is proved that the generator matrix GGG of an ideal linear code realizing a complete ttt-partite 222-uniform hypergraph access structure must have a rank of 222. Simultaneously, a novel method for constructing an ideal secret sharing scheme that realizes such access structures is proposed. Building on this foundation, the case of complete ttt-partite 222-uniform hypergraphs is extended to complete ttt-partite kkk-uniform hypergraphs, and a method for constructing ideal secret sharing schemes to realize them is provided.
