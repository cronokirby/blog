Abstract

We will present in this paper two new (post-Quantum) multivariate signature schemes, one with short signature, called HFELL’HFE_{LL’}HFELL’​, and one with even shorter ("ultra-short") signatures, called D−HFELL’D-HFE_{LL’}D−HFELL’​. These two schemes are variants of the HFE scheme (one of the most studied multivariate schemes) with a new and specific use of the LL’LL’LL’ perturbation. In the last years, HFE suffered new attacks, specially MinRank attacks, but thanks to our specific LL’LL’LL’ perturbation, our schemes resist all know attacks.
