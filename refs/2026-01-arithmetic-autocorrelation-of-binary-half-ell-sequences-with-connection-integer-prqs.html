(2026-01) Arithmetic autocorrelation of binary half--ell-sequences with connection integer p^{r}q^{s}

Abstract

Half-ℓ\ellℓ-sequences, as a extension of ℓ\ellℓ-sequences, have attracted research interest over the past decade. The arithmetic correlation of half-ℓ\ellℓ-sequences is known for connection integers of the form prp^rpr. In this paper, we extend this result by deriving the arithmetic correlation for half-ℓ\ellℓ-sequences with connection integers of the form prqsp^r q^s prqs. The results indicate that when p≡−1(mod8)p\equiv -1 \pmod{8}p≡−1(mod8) and q≡±3(mod8)q\equiv \pm 3 \pmod{8}q≡±3(mod8), the arithmetic autocorrelation can be determined by the number of odd integers in the cyclic subgroup generated by 222 modulo ppp.
