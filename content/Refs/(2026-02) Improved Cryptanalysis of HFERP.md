# Abstract

> In this paper we introduce a new attack on the multivariate
> encryption scheme HFERP, a big field scheme including an extra variable
> set, additional equations of the UOV or Rainbow shape as well as addi-
> tional random polynomials. Our attack brings several parameter sets well
> below their claimed security levels. The attack combines novel methods
> applicable to multivariate schemes with multiple equation types with in-
> sights from the Simple Attack that broke Rainbow in early 2022, though
> interestingly the technique is applied in an orthogonal way. In addition to
> this attack, we apply support minors techniques on a MinRank instance
> drawing coefficients from the big field, which was effective against other
> multivariate big field schemes. This work demonstrates that there exist
> previously unknown impacts of the above works well beyond the scope
> in which they were derived.
