# Abstract

> We present an efficient identity-based blind signature
> scheme built upon the SM9 Chinese national cryptographic
> standard. Our scheme achieves weak one-more unforgeability under
> the $q$-SDH assumption, and computational blindness in
> the random oracle model (ROM), both proved rigorously.
> The weak security model, while restricting adversaries
> from querying signatures on the target identity, remains
> sufficient for critical applications including electronic
> cash, anonymous credentials, and privacy-preserving
> voting systems. We provide rigorous security proofs and demonstrate
> significant performance improvements over existing
> SM9-based blind signature schemes, eliminating all
> online pairing computations through pre-computation.
> Our construction maintains full compatibility with the
> SM9 standard, facilitating deployment in
> certificate-free environments.
