This page is intended to collect information about developing emulators for the SNES.

Architecture

CPU

Uses a Ricoh 5A22 CPU, which is based on the Notes/WDC W65C816S, in turn an evolution of the classic Notes/6502 ISA, with 16 bit functionality.

Startup

The CPU, upon initialization, will go to the reset vector at fffc
