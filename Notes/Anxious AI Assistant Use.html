Anxious AI Assistant Use

The use of AI assistants in an anxious way. Perhaps "avoidant" might be the better term. Sometimes people use chat assistants as a way to avoid other obligations in their life, or to lessen the cognitive load preventing them from starting a task. In the worst form, this is like social media addiction, in that the user engages in a negative and all-consuming habit.
